Interior estimate

Each room is a section. Inside a room, click + Wall for each of the four sides and + Ceiling for the top — a hall gives you 4 walls plus 1 ceiling. Every wall has its own size, its own deductions, and its own putty / primer / paint settings, so one wall can be putty-only while another takes two coats.

Same room-and-wall layout for outside faces. Group walls by elevation (front, back, sides) into a "room", click a wall per face, deduct windows, grilles and garage doors. Exterior coverage runs lower by default since rough surfaces drink more paint.
